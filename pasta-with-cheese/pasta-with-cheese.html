Pasta with cheese

Ingredients:

  • Pasta, 400g
  • Cheese, 100g
  • Table salt, 1/2 teaspoon
  1. Put pasta in 500mL of boiling water
  2. Add salt to the water
  3. Boil for 7 minutes
  4. Take out the pasta from the water after boiling, rinse it with cold water to prevent clumping
  5. Put it in plate and grate cheese on top
  6. Enjoy!
