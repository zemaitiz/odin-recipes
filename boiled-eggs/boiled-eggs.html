Boiled eggs

A serving of boiled eggs, with respective boiling times

Ingredients:

  • Eggs, 4
  • Mayo, 2 spoons
  1. Put eggs in 500mL of water and let it boil
  2. Depending on desired hardness of your eggs, boil for a set amount of time (image above
  3. Take out the eggs from the water after boiling, let them cool
  4. Remove the shells
  5. Enjoy with mayo!
