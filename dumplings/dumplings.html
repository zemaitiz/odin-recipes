Dumplings

Ingredients:

  • Dumplings, 400g
  • Sour cream, two spoons
  • Table salt, 1/2 teaspoon
  1. Boil about 500mL of water
  2. Add table salt to water
  3. When the water is boiling, put frozen dumplings in it
  4. Stir until the dumplings emerge to the surface
  5. Boil for 5-7 minutes
  6. Remove dumplings from water and put in a plate
  7. Add sour cream on top
  8. Enjoy!
